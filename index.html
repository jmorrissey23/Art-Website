About Tricia

Tricia McKenzie Morrissey fell in love with painting and drawing as a small child. She was greatly encouraged by her mother who was an artist and an occasional window decorator. Tricia graduated from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and started her career focusing on painting while experimenting with a wide variety of mediums and subject matter. She enjoys painting and is either doodling or drawing whenever she can get her hands on a pencil and paper. Tricia lives in a suburb of Chicago with her husband, two kids and dog.
